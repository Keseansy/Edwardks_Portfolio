Graphic Design

The aim of this project was to create packaging for a new candy product.

I spent a good amount of time in the sketching and planning phases and eventually decided on the Pixel Bytes idea.

From there, I created digital iterations of pixels and cube designs until I decided that an isometric grid system would work best for the design. It gave the packaging that wow factor it needed to pop.

The aim of this project was to create a series of textbook covers that represented the three areas of bioremediation: microbial remediation, phytoremediation, and mycoremediation.

The outcome was meant to have roots in science while retaining a sense of visual intrigue and modernism.

The project entailed extensive research into bioremediation so that I would have a deeper understanding of the subject when designing for it.
